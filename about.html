Leaf is a modern cloud-synced text editor built for writers and developers. Vim keybindings, Markdown preview, Google Drive auto-save, and full offline support — all in your browser.

Start Writing Now Explore Features

Everything You Need to Focus

Leaf automates file management so you can concentrate on what matters — your writing.

Google Drive Auto-Sync

Every keystroke is automatically saved to your Google Drive. No manual save needed — your work is always backed up and accessible from any device.

Full Offline Support

Lost your connection? Leaf seamlessly switches to local storage. When you're back online, changes sync silently in the background. Zero interruption.

Vim Keybindings

Toggle Vim mode with one click. Navigate with H/J/K/L, use powerful commands, and enjoy the efficiency of Vim — right in your browser.

Markdown Preview

Write in Markdown and instantly preview beautifully rendered output including code blocks, tables, Mermaid diagrams, and math equations.

Smart Categories

Organize your sheets into folders that sync with Google Drive. Move documents between categories with drag-and-drop or the management dialog.

Multi-Tab Editing

Open multiple sheets simultaneously and switch between them with tabs. Use Alt+[ / Alt+] to navigate tabs, or Alt+W to close. On mobile, access the sheet list panel with a single tap.

PWA & Mobile Ready

Install Leaf as a Progressive Web App on any device. Touch-optimized with swipe gestures for deleting and managing sheets on mobile.

Syntax Highlighting

Over 100 programming languages supported. Change the file extension to instantly switch highlighting — JavaScript, Rust, Python, Go, and more.

9 Languages

The entire interface adapts to your browser language. English, Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Spanish, German, French, Italian, and Dutch are fully supported.

Privacy First

Leaf is a client-side application. Your files never pass through our servers — they go directly between your browser and Google Drive. We store nothing.

Get Started in Seconds

No installation, no configuration files. Just sign in with your Google account and start writing.

Open Leaf

Visit the app in your browser and sign in with your Google account. Cloud sync is enabled automatically, and you can start writing right away.

Start Writing

Create a new sheet and begin typing. Your content auto-saves as you type — to Google Drive when online, or locally when offline.

Access Anywhere

Your documents are always available on Google Drive. Open Leaf on any device, sign in, and continue right where you left off.

Keyboard-Driven Workflow

Power users can navigate the entire app without ever touching the mouse.

Alt + NCreate a new sheet
Alt + MOpen sheet management dialog
Alt + OOpen a local file
Alt + SForce save to Google Drive
Alt + LToggle Markdown / code preview
Alt + FFind and replace in document
Alt + [Switch to left tab
Alt + ]Switch to right tab
Alt + WClose current tab
Alt + -Decrease font size
Alt + =Increase font size
